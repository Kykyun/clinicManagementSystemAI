### 2. PATIENT MODULE

Create a **Patient** module with the following features:

   * Generate a visit record for each registration (date, doctor, reason, status).

2. **Consultation**

   * For each visit, allow doctors to record:

     * Chief complaint, history of present illness, examination findings, diagnosis, treatment plan.
     * Prescribed medicines (linked to Medicine & Stock from Setup module).
   * Save consultation notes and allow edit history.

3. **Appointment**

   * Appointment scheduling for patients:

     * Choose doctor, date, time, reason.
     * Status: scheduled, completed, cancelled, no-show.

4. **Payment**

   * Link to visits and consultations.
   * Record payment amount, payment method (cash, card, e-wallet, etc.), invoice number, date.
   * Support partial payment, outstanding balance.

5. **Doctor’s Fee**

   * Configure per-doctor consultation fees.
   * Automatically calculate doctor’s fee based on visits and consultations.

6. **Antenatal**

   * Register antenatal patients.
   * Track antenatal visits, vitals (BP, weight, fundal height), lab tests, scan results, and expected delivery date.

7. **FOMEMA**

   * Module for foreign worker medical examinations.

8. **Immunization**

   * Track vaccines given to patients.
   * Fields: vaccine type, batch number, date given, next due date, administered by (staff).

9. **Lab & X-ray Results**

   * Upload and store lab and X-ray results (file upload + structured data).
   * Link results to patient and visit.
   * Status: pending, completed, reviewed.

10. **Medical Checkup**

* For general medical checkups (e.g. pre-employment, insurance).
* Standard form: history, physical exam, lab tests, conclusion, fitness status.

11. **Medical History**

* Consolidated view of all past visits, diagnoses, medications, allergies, lab results.

12. **Med. History**

* Separate but related to point 11: focus on **Medication History** (drugs prescribed, dosages, start/end dates).

13. **Referral Letter**

* Generate printable referral letters to specialists/hospitals.
* Auto-fill patient data, doctor details, reason for referral, findings.

14. **Quarantine Slip**

* Generate quarantine slips for infectious diseases.
* Includes patient info, diagnosis, quarantine period, doctor details.
